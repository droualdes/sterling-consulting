Sterling Consulting to Consultant Relationships

It's simple. We get to know you: your skills, your personality, your goals. We develop long-term relationships with you so that we can do the best job in matching your skills with the right Client. We always fully disclose and discuss the opportunity and agree on an hourly rate before we present you to a client. We always get your permission before we send your resume to the client. We coach you for each interview.

We provide you with candid feedback from the client. We stay involved with you and the client from start to finish on each assignment providing you with support and advise to ensure your success. We respond to your emails and your phone calls within 24 hours. We thank you for your service and when you bring new clients to the firm. We stay in touch!
